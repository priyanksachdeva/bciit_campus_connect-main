About BCIIT

Our Institution

BCIIT has been a pioneer in technical education since its establishment in 1995.

We are committed to providing quality education that combines rigorous academics with practical experience. Our campus spans over 25 acres with state-of-the-art facilities, including modern laboratories, a well-stocked library, and excellent sports infrastructure.

With over 150 faculty members and 3000+ students, we create an environment that fosters innovation, research, and holistic development.
